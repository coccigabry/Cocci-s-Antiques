About

Beautiful antiques. Since 1986

Located on the ancient and historic street of Via Don Giovanni Verità, we’re in the bulls-eye of Voltri borough centre and its outstanding premium shopping district. Our neighbours are other jewellers, watch-sellers, art dealers, and high-end clothing and homewares shops.

We are a full-service antiques centre that offers a mind-boggling range of items from all ages and all corners of the world. This means that, as a visitor, you’re likely to find exactly what you’re looking for and know that the item you find is of the highest quality.

Once you visit our centre, chances are that we’ll see you again.
